Poster Session

Reward

Registration

  1. To register for the poster session, choose the "POSTER" option at registration time.

Instructions

  1. We will provide you with a wall, some tape and pins to hang your poster.
  2. We won't provide printing service for your poster. You will have to come with your poster already printed.
  3. A money prize will be awarded to the poster elected as best poster by the poster jury.
