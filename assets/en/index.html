CARaDOC stands for Careers and Doctors. It is a day of exchange between young researchers (PhD Students and PostDocs) of Paris Saclay University and companies.
After last year’s successful event (J-RED 2019 with 300 participants and 25 companies) and considering the specific circumstances related to the COVID-19 crisis, we decided to have a virtual event on November 18, 2020.
That is why this year it will be a virtual career convention where employers and PhD students of Paris Saclay meet in a virtual environment, using chat rooms, teleconferencing and/or email to exchange information about job openings, company culture, etc. Companies can be matched with potential applicants as well as search for candidates using the platform.

Statistics about University Paris-Saclay

With 275 laboratories shared with the CEA, CNRS, IHES, INRAE, INRIA, INSERM, Onéra, Université Paris-Saclay represents 13% of the French research potential.
